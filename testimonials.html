Headshots - All testimonials are good and if all else fails, a text only testimonial is better than no testimonial. But 65% of the population are visual learners and testimonials that include a photo are much more authentic. If you can’t persuade your client to provide a headshot, video, or candid photo (see below) you may be able to persuade them to allow you to use their company logo alongside their testimonial instead.
